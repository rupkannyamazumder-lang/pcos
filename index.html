About PCOS

PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) is a hormonal and metabolic disorder that affects women of reproductive age. It occurs when the ovaries produce higher-than-normal levels of androgens.

PCOS impacts multiple systems: Reproductive, Hormonal, Metabolic, and significantly, Mental Health. Management focuses on regulating periods, improving insulin sensitivity, and supporting your emotional wellness.

Note: PCOS is not your fault. It does not define your worth or future.
