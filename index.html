What is PCOS?

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal condition affecting how ovaries work. It is common, manageable, and you are never alone.

Hormonal Imbalance

PCOS involves higher androgen levels which may affect periods, skin, mood, and emotions.

Mental Health Impact

PCOS can affect self-esteem, anxiety, and mood swings. Your feelings are valid and deserve care.

Myths vs Facts

Myth: PCOS means infertility
Fact: Many women conceive naturally or with support.
