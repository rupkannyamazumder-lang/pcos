PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) is a hormonal and metabolic disorder where the ovaries produce higher-than-normal levels of androgens (male hormones). This interferes with regular ovulation, causing periods to be irregular or absent.

PCOS is a whole-body condition. It impacts the Reproductive system (cysts, irregular periods), Hormonal system (excess androgens), Metabolic system (weight gain, insulin resistance), and Skin/Hair.
