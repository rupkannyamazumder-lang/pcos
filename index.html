What is PCOS?

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome is a hormonal and metabolic disorder affecting women of reproductive age. It occurs when ovaries produce higher-than-normal levels of androgens.

It impacts reproductive health, metabolic balance, and mental well-being. Management is not a destination, but a beautiful lifestyle journey.
