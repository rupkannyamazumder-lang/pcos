PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) is a hormonal and metabolic disorder affecting women of reproductive age.

It occurs when ovaries produce higher-than-normal levels of androgens. This disrupts regular ovulation, causing periods to become irregular, delayed, or absent.
