Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal and metabolic disorder that affects women of reproductive age. It occurs when the ovaries produce higher-than-normal levels of androgens (male hormones).

Hormonal Imbalance: Ovulation does not happen regularly, making periods irregular or absent. Insulin Resistance: The body doesn't use insulin effectively, leading to weight shifts and fatigue.

Reassurance: PCOS is not your fault. It doesn't define your worth. With the right care, you are capable of a radiant life.
