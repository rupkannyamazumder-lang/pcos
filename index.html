PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) is a hormonal and metabolic disorder that affects women of reproductive age.

It occurs when the ovaries produce higher-than-normal levels of androgens, which interferes with normal ovulation. Because ovulation does not happen regularly, periods become irregular, delayed, or absent.

üî¨ Main Causes: Hormonal Imbalance, Insulin Resistance, Genetics, and Chronic Inflammation.

PCOS impacts multiple systems: Reproductive (Irregularity), Metabolic (Weight/Insulin), and Mental Health (Anxiety/Depression).

Reassurance: PCOS is not your fault. It does not define your worth. With the right care, it is manageable.
